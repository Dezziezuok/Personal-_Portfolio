More About Me

I'm an aspiring software engineer, but beyond the screen, at the heart of everything I do is my
faith. As a Christian and Nigerian-American, I carry a strong sense of resilience, and purpose,
rooted in faith, cultivated by culture, and driven by the desire to make meaningful impacts.

When I’m not buried under books or writing code, you’ll probably find me crocheting something
cozy, getting a good workout in at the gym, fine-tuning my budget (yes, I genuinely enjoy it), or

Hence the correlation between coding and crocheting loops— both require creativity, patience,
and a love for building something meaningful, one stitch or line at a time.
